# Buffalo Chicken Bacon Mac & Cheese (High Protein Meal Prep)

## Ingredients

### Protein & Base
- 4 lb chicken breast (raw, skinless)
- 8 slices uncured center cut bacon (Hempler's, cooked & chopped)
- 16 oz cavatappi pasta (DeCecco)
- 2 cups low sodium chicken broth

### Cheese Sauce
- 800 g fat-free cottage cheese (Kroger)
- 200 g 1/3 reduced fat cream cheese (Philadelphia)
- 200 g premium cheddar (Rico's)
- 200 g reduced fat cheddar
- 2 packets Kraft mac & cheese powder
- ½ cup Frank's RedHot buffalo sauce (or more for extra heat)

### Seasonings
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper

---

## Directions

1. **Cook Bacon** — Pan fry until crispy. Drain, chop, set aside.

2. **Slow Cooker Base** — Add chicken breasts, chicken broth, and buffalo sauce. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Cover and cook:
   - LOW 6–7 hours, OR
   - HIGH 3–4 hours

3. **Shred Chicken** — Remove chicken, shred with forks, return to slow cooker juices.

4. **Cook Pasta** — Boil cavatappi until al dente. Drain and set aside.

5. **Make Cheese Sauce** — Blend cottage cheese, cream cheese, both cheddars, and mac & cheese powder until smooth. Add a splash of broth if needed.

6. **Combine** — Stir pasta into shredded chicken. Pour in cheese sauce, mix until fully coated. Fold in bacon. Adjust buffalo sauce/salt to taste.

7. **Serve** — Top with green onions or extra buffalo drizzle if desired.
