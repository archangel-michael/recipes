BUFFALO CHICKEN BACON MAC & CHEESE (High Protein Meal Prep) 🧀🔥

Makes 10 servings
Per serving (approx.):
602 calories • 67g protein • 43g carbs • 16g fat
⸻
🛒 INGREDIENTS
• 4 lb chicken breast (raw, skinless)
• 16 oz cavatappi pasta (DeCecco)
• 800 g fat-free cottage cheese (Kroger)
• 200 g 1/3 reduced fat cream cheese (Philadelphia)
• 200 g premium cheddar (Rico’s)
• 200 g reduced fat cheddar
• 8 slices uncured center cut bacon (Hempler’s, cooked & chopped)
• ½ cup Frank’s RedHot buffalo sauce (or more for extra heat)
• 2 packets Kraft mac & cheese powder
• 2 cups low sodium chicken broth

⸻

🧂 SEASONINGS
• 1 tsp garlic powder
• 1 tsp onion powder
• 1 tsp smoked paprika
• 1 tsp salt
• ½ tsp black pepper

⸻

👨‍🍳 DIRECTIONS
Cook Bacon
Pan fry 8 slices of bacon until crispy. Drain, chop, and set aside.
Slow Cooker Base
Add chicken breasts, chicken broth, and buffalo sauce to slow cooker.
Season with garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
Cover and cook:

• LOW 6–7 hours OR
• HIGH 3–4 hours
until chicken shreds easily.

Shred Chicken
Remove chicken, shred with forks, then return to slow cooker juices.
Cook Pasta
Boil cavatappi until al dente. Drain and set aside.
Make Cheese Sauce
In a blender, combine:

• cottage cheese
• cream cheese
• premium cheddar
• reduced fat cheddar
• mac & cheese powder

Blend until smooth and creamy (add a splash of broth if needed).
Combine Everything
Stir cooked pasta into shredded chicken mixture.
Pour in cheese sauce and mix until fully coated.
Fold in chopped bacon.
Taste and adjust buffalo sauce or salt as needed.
Serve
Top with green onions or extra buffalo drizzle if desired.
