# Honey Chipotle Chicken Quesadillas

## Ingredients

### Chicken Filling
- 40 oz (1135g) chicken breast
- 1 Tbsp (15g) garlic salt
- 1 Tbsp (7g) black pepper
- 7 oz (200g) canned chipotle peppers in adobo
- 1 lime, juiced
- 2 Tbsp (40g) honey
- 6 cups (670g) fat free mozzarella, shredded
- Fresh chives (10g), chopped
- 10 low calorie tortillas

### Chipotle Sauce
- 2 cups (450g) 0% fat cottage cheese
- 3 Tbsp (45g) light mayo
- 3 Tbsp (50g) minced chipotle peppers
- 1 lime, juiced
- 0.5 Tbsp (7g) garlic salt
- 0.5 Tbsp (3g) black pepper
- 2 Tbsp (40g) honey
- 0.5 cup (120g) fat free milk

---

## Instructions

### 1. Make the chipotle sauce
Blend all chipotle sauce ingredients (cottage cheese, light mayo, minced chipotles, lime juice, garlic salt, black pepper, honey, milk) until completely smooth. Refrigerate until needed.

### 2. Cook the chicken
Season chicken breast with garlic salt and black pepper. Cook in a skillet over medium-high heat or in a slow cooker until fully cooked through. Add canned chipotle peppers, lime juice, and honey. Shred the chicken and toss to coat in the sauce. Cook down any excess liquid.

### 3. Assemble the quesadillas
Lay a tortilla flat. Spread a layer of chipotle sauce over half the tortilla. Add a portion of shredded chicken, a generous layer of fat free mozzarella, and fresh chives. Fold the tortilla in half.

### 4. Cook the quesadillas
Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Cook each quesadilla 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown and the cheese is melted. Repeat for all 10 tortillas.

### 5. Serve or freeze
Slice each quesadilla and serve immediately, or let cool and freeze.

---

## Freezing Instructions

1. Wrap each quesadilla individually in aluminum foil or place in an airtight freezer bag.
2. Freeze for up to 3 months.
3. Reheat in an air fryer or skillet directly from frozen.

---

## Notes

- Low calorie tortillas keep carbs down significantly — Mission Carb Balance or similar work well
- Fat free mozzarella melts differently than full-fat; cook on medium heat to avoid rubbery texture
- The chipotle sauce also works as a dip or sandwich spread
